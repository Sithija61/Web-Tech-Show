About Us

WEB TECH SHOW is the premier web technology conference in Sri Lanka, bringing together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from across the country and beyond. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and connect the web community through high-quality content and networking opportunities.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small local meetup to one of the most anticipated tech events in the region, featuring world-class speakers and cutting-edge content.
